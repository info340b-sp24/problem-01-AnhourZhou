Langa Hasegawa


Langa is a character from anime SK8_the_Infinity. A half-Japanese student who transferred to Okinawa from Canada, with his mother, and ended up transferring into Reki's (His friend) class. Growing up as a snowboarder, Langa quickly learns to transfer his skills into skateboarding when he jumps into his first "S" run.


Reasons I admire him:

  • His great passion and persistence to the his hobbies that encourage me
  • His high level of snowboarding and skateboarding impresses me and becomes the role model I want to learn from
  • He is so pretty!
